Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, booking process, and travel policies.

Booking & Payments

You can book directly through our website by selecting your destination, choosing dates, and completing the secure checkout process. Our team will contact you within 24 hours to confirm details and provide additional information.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. Payment plans are available for bookings over $2,000 with a 30% deposit required.

A 30% deposit is required to secure your booking. The remaining balance is due 45 days before departure for international trips and 30 days for domestic trips.

Cancellations & Changes

Free cancellation within 48 hours of booking. After that: 60+ days before departure (10% fee), 30-59 days (25% fee), 15-29 days (50% fee), less than 15 days (75% fee). Some suppliers may have stricter policies.

Yes, modifications are possible subject to availability and supplier policies. Changes may incur fees ranging from $50-200 depending on the type of modification and timing.

We understand emergencies happen. We'll work with you and our suppliers to minimize fees. We strongly recommend travel insurance to protect against unforeseen circumstances.

Travel & Services

We partner with leading insurance providers to offer comprehensive travel insurance options. Coverage includes trip cancellation, medical emergencies, lost luggage, and more. Insurance is optional but highly recommended.

Absolutely! We specialize in customized experiences. Contact our travel experts to discuss your preferences, interests, and budget. We handle everything from solo adventures to large group trips.

Our packages typically include accommodation, transportation, guided tours, some meals, and 24/7 support. Specific inclusions vary by destination and package type. All details are clearly outlined in your booking confirmation.

Yes! Groups of 8+ people receive special pricing. We also offer customized group experiences for corporate retreats, family reunions, and special celebrations. Contact us for a personalized quote.

Support & Safety

We provide 24/7 emergency support throughout your journey. You'll have direct contact with our local representatives and access to our emergency hotline for any assistance needed.

Safety is our top priority. We work only with vetted local partners, provide detailed safety briefings, monitor travel advisories, and maintain comprehensive emergency protocols for all destinations.

We accommodate various dietary restrictions and accessibility needs. Please inform us during booking so we can make appropriate arrangements with hotels, restaurants, and activity providers.

Our travel experts are here to help. Contact us for personalized assistance with your travel planning.
